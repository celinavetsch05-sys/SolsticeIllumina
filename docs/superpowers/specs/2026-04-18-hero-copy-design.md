## Target Audience

Founders, personal brands, and small businesses that care about aesthetics and positioning. Willing to invest in thoughtful design. Not looking for the cheapest option.

## Differentiator

Celina handles both brand identity **and** web development — one person, start to finish. Most founders have to hire a designer and a developer separately. This is the core message.

## What the Copy Must Do

- Be clear within 3 seconds what is being offered
- Sound premium but not vague
- Avoid phrases that could apply to any designer
- Focus on outcome and positioning, not just "design"
- Speak to the client, not about the designer

## Hero Section — Final Copy

**Eyebrow (unchanged)**
`Brand Identity | Web Design | Content`

**Headline**
`Brand identity and web design — one person, start to finish.`

**Subheadline**
`Most founders split this between a designer and a developer. I handle both, so your brand stays consistent from logo to live site.`

**CTA Buttons**
- Primary: `View Projects` (scrolls to portfolio)
- Secondary: `Start a Project` (scrolls to contact)

**Supporting line** (below buttons, small/muted)
`Identity, website, and content built as one system — not separate deliverables.`
