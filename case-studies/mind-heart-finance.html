The Brief

Mind Heart Finance Consulting needed a complete brand identity to establish credibility in the competitive finance space — logo, a trust-building color system, target audience analysis, and Instagram content that educates clients on complex topics like market volatility and long-term investing. The deliverable covered everything from brand strategy through to ready-to-post social templates.

What I Learned

Finance content works best when it turns anxiety into clarity. The Instagram posts I designed used simple visuals and direct copy to help clients see market volatility as context, not crisis — the same principle that drove every color and layout decision across the whole brand system.
